Meshrender Documentation¶

Welcome to the documentation for meshrender! This module, which is build on top of PyOpenGL, is designed to make it easy to render images of 3D scenes in pure Python. It supports a scene abstraction and allows users to specify material properties, camera intrinsics, and lighting.
